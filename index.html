Arcade iPhone Game

Snake Classic+

Snake Classic+ is a fast, focused take on the arcade original. Guide the snake, chase a higher score, and jump straight back into the next run with responsive controls and a clean retro presentation.

What It Is

A score-chasing snake game built for short sessions. Play with swipe controls or on-screen controls, restart quickly, and push for a better run.

  • Classic snake gameplay with a polished modern feel
  • Fast restarts and clear game states
  • Simple, readable interface built for phone screens

Privacy

Snake Classic+ does not require an account and does not collect personal data from players through the app.

  • Gameplay preferences and related settings may be stored locally on your device
  • No in-app account system
  • No third-party advertising or tracking, if the released build remains unchanged
